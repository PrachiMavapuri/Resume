ABOUT US




Dreamt up in a living room in 2016 by a girl who loved pretty pumps, we still stay true to this aesthetic through our classic-meets-contemporary designs. While we’ve outgrown the living room, we’re still a small team committed to providing customers with beautiful fashion in inclusive sizes, made ethically by factories around the world.

Give a girl the right shoes, and she will conquer the world,"
-Marilyn Monroe


The right pair of shoes, especially heels, help bolster a woman’s confidence, but finding the sweet spot between style and comfort is often a tall order. Many women give up one for the other: a pair of sexy heels that pinch your toes, or a comfortable pair of flats that has nothing by way of style going for it.


We found this to be troubling, and wanted to have both: plenty of style with oodles of comfort. In 2018, we started Ready to wear to provide women with stylish footwear that did not compromise on comfort.
